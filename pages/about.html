Welcome to SkillsUSA

SkillsUSA is a national organization that empowers students to become world-class workers and leaders in their respective fields. At Metea Valley, we strive to foster excellence in career and technical education.

What is SkillsUSA?

SkillsUSA is a nonprofit organization that helps students develop the technical, leadership, and professional skills required to excel in their careers. Through competitions, leadership activities, and industry partnerships, SkillsUSA students gain real-world experience that prepares them for success after graduation.

Our Impact

SkillsUSA has made a significant impact on the students of Metea Valley, helping them succeed in technical fields through real-world experiences. Through competitions and leadership opportunities, students build skills that lead to greater success in their careers and beyond.

  • Over 70 students participated in the SkillsUSA state competitions last year.
  • More than 20 students competed at the SkillsUSA National level!
  • Our alumni are pursuing successful careers with industry leaders and top universities.
